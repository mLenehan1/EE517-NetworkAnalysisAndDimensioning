Re-formulating the problem as a modified flow-allocation problem results in the
following formulation:

Minimize:

\begin{align*}
	z
\end{align*}

Subject to:

\begin{align*}
	x_{1,1} + x_{1,2} &= h_1 \\
	x_{2,1} + x_{2,2} &= h_2 \\
	x_{1,1} + x_{2,1} &\le c_1 + z \\
	x_{1,2} + x_{2,1} &\le c_2 + z \\
	x_{1,2} + x_{2,2} &\le c_3 + z \\
	x_{1,1},x_{1,2},x_{2,1},x_{2,2} &\ge 0
\end{align*}
